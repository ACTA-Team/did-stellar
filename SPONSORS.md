# Sponsors

`did-stellar` is developed by [ACTA](https://acta.build) with support from the
organizations listed below.

## Stellar Community Fund

The [Stellar Community Fund](https://communityfund.stellar.org) awarded ACTA a
grant to build the `did:stellar` method: the SDK, the registry contract, and the
public HTTP resolver in this repository.

- **Award:** [Stellar Community Fund submission](https://communityfund.stellar.org/submissions/recUBbVLf5Vj8oEW0)
- **Scope:** `did:stellar` v0.1 specification, `@acta-team/did-stellar` SDK,
  `did-stellar-registry` Soroban contract, and the DIF Universal Resolver
  compatible service at [`did.acta.build`](https://did.acta.build).

Funding covers development only. The project remains open source under
[Apache-2.0](LICENSE), and the resolver is trust-minimised: it holds no keys and
requires no authentication.

## Supporting the project

If you or your organization would like to sponsor continued work on
